One Stablecoin For All

DUSD = $USD and Better

DUSD is the World’s first synthetic stablecoin for reliable, secure, fast money transfer,
diversification, storage and wealth allocation

What We Offers

Simple and Direct
Trust Model

DUSD is indexed to a basket of selected stablecoins of superior quality with high transparency, and liquidity, including USDC, TUSD, PAX and DAI, 1:1 pegged to USD through smart contract.

Diversification and
derisking

DUSD caps single point of failure risk at 30% max, diversifying stablecoin holders’ counterparty risks significantly and saving the hassle of holding multiple products.

Decentralization
with Scalability

Unlike fiat-back, collateral-back or algorithmic stablecoins, our protocol is inherently extendable and programmable, allowing unlimited scalability, permissionless and open access with no central operation or intervention.

Fast and Immediate

DUSD is an ERC20 token built on top of Ethereum network, it is operating 24/7 with immediate clearance and settlement. Money moving in light speed.

High Transparency and
Auditability

Our DUSD and its constituent stablecoins are recorded in the Ethereum ledger with 100% auditable transparency.

Multi Insurance and
Interest Earning

We have protocol-embedded insurance pool and platform token to act as shock buffer in the event of systemic risk. We will incorporate other DeFi platforms or build our own financial market, to allow DUSD holders to earn yield.
